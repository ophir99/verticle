Voice & Video

video_library

The Voice / Video Connectivity consists of the IPTV, IP Telephony and Video Conference. IPTV is a TV system based around the use of Internet Protocol which enhances the quality of the transmitted channels and provides a maximum utilization of the network. The IP telephony is using the Internet Protocol's packet-switched connections to exchange voice, fax, and other forms of information; while the Video Conferencing allows two or more locations to communicate by simultaneous two-way video and audio transmissions.
